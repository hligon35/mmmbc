Facility Rental

Information about renting facilities at Mt. Moriah Missionary Baptist Church will be available here. This will include details about available spaces, rental fees, policies, and a contact person for inquiries.

We are in the process of updating our facility rental guidelines and application forms. Please contact the church office for the most current information.

Our doors are always open to you. If you are without a church home, we hope you will unite with the Mt. Moriah family. Wherever you go, may you always feel the presence of God and may the blessings of faithful worship bring peace and joy to your heart.
