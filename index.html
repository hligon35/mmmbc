Frequently Asked Questions

What denomination is Mt. Moriah Baptist Church?

We are a Missionary Baptist Church which means, we are to go out into the world, preaching, teaching and bringing others to Christ.

Where are you located?

Our church is located at 1201 South 8th Street in Paducah, KY. We would love for you to visit us!

How do I contact you?

You can contact us by phone at (270) 443-3714 or by email at mtmoriahmbc@att.net or follow us on social media.

What does your worship service look like?

We have an order of service which starts at 11:00 a.m. every Sunday morning including singing, praying and preaching.

Are there any services that help me learn more about God?

Yes, we offer several opportunities for spiritual growth, including Sunday Discipleship Training for youth and adults, and weekly Bible Study. Please check our "Worship Times" and "Weekly Ministry Times" sections for more details.
